Orientation

To ensure our clients are well-prepared and equipped with essential knowledge, Prime Korean Language and Review Center Incorporated conducts comprehensive orientations. During these orientations, we emphasize the pivotal role of the Korean language as a key requirement for employment in South Korea. It is important to note that Prime KLC is not a recruitment agency but a dedicated provider of Korean language tutorial services. Our goal is to guide and support our clients on their language learning journey, enabling them to successfully pass the EPS-TOPIK exam. We also inform our clients that only the Department of Migrant Workers (DMW) has the authority to facilitate their departure to South Korea. Through our orientations, we provide step-by-step guidance on the application process for working in South Korea, empowering our clients with the necessary information to navigate the journey ahead.

Actual Class

At Prime KLC, our actual classes are meticulously designed to provide our students with ample time and comprehensive training to master the Korean language. Our curriculum encompasses 10 days of basic Korean language training, followed by 7 days of intermediate training and 7 days of advanced training. Additionally, we offer a dedicated 1-month review period leading up to the e-EPS-TOPIKexam. What sets us apart is that even after the completion of regular classes and reviews, we provide our students with lifetime unlimited sit-ins, allowing them to continue refining their language skills until they successfully pass the EPS-TOPIK exam and secure employment in South Korea. This unparalleled level of support is our unwavering commitment to our students' success, ensuring their journey is filled with opportunities for growth without any additional charges
