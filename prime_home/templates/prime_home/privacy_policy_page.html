PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to Prime Korean Language and Review Center Inc.

Prime Korean Language Tutorial Services (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website Prime Korean Language (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, protect, and disclose your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. Please read it carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

This Privacy Policy should be read alongside our Terms and Conditions and Service Agreement, which govern all use of our website and services.

1. Introduction

Prime Korean Language and Review Center Inc. is committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes the types of data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding that data.

By accessing our website or using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. We also encourage you to review our Terms and Conditions, which govern your use of the Service, and together with this Privacy Policy, form our Agreement with you.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information in order to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Data
    Personal data refers to information that can directly or indirectly identify you. We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our services:

    • Name

    • Email address

    • Phone number

    • Address

    • Other relevant details for communication and service delivery

    This information is collected when you register, inquire about services, or communicate with us.

  • Usage Data
    We automatically collect information regarding how you interact with our website or use our services. This may include:

    • IP address

    • Browser type and version

    • Pages visited on our website

    • Time and date of visits

    • Duration of visits

    • User interactions with the site (clicks, scrolls, etc.)

    Usage data is essential to help us improve the website experience and provide a personalized service.

  • Cookies and Tracking Data
    Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files that store data such as your preferences and help us analyze website traffic. You may control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling cookies may affect your user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services
    Personal data helps us offer personalized language tutorial services, tailored to your specific learning needs. It also allows us to track your progress and provide feedback to help you achieve your language learning goals.

  • To Communicate with You
    Your contact information allows us to respond to your inquiries, provide support, and keep you informed about important updates, changes, or news regarding our services.

  • To Enhance Website Functionality
    Usage data and cookies help us analyze website traffic, identify trends, and improve website functionality. This also helps us personalize your user experience and optimize website navigation.

  • For Marketing and Promotional Purposes
    With your consent, we may use your email address or other contact information to send marketing materials, service promotions, and updates. You have the option to opt-out of these communications at any time.

  • For Security and Fraud Prevention
    We may use your data to monitor website activity for security purposes, including preventing unauthorized access, fraud, and other illegal activities.

  • For Legal Compliance
    We may use your data to comply with applicable laws and regulations, enforce our agreements, and protect the rights of Prime Korean Language and Review Center Inc. and its users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

For example, personal information provided during registration is retained for the duration of your use of our services, and for a reasonable period thereafter, to facilitate any future engagements or inquiries.

Usage data may be retained for analytical purposes but is typically kept only for a limited time unless needed for improving the website or complying with legal obligations.

5. How We Share Your Data

We may share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our services, such as web hosting, email communication, customer support, and analytics. These third parties are only allowed to use your data to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

We may also share your data in the following circumstances:

  • To Comply with Legal Obligations
    If required by law, we may disclose your personal data to governmental authorities or law enforcement agencies.

  • To Protect Our Rights
    We may disclose personal data to protect the rights, property, and safety of Prime Korean Language and Review Center Inc. and its users.

  • In Business Transfers
    If our company undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Security of Your Data

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These include physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. However, despite our efforts, no data transmission over the internet or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain conditions.

  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.

  • Data Portability: You may request the transfer of your data to another service provider in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve your experience when you visit our website. These files allow us to recognize you and remember your preferences. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Please note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

We also use analytics tools to track user behavior on our website, which helps us improve the content and layout.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through email or a prominent notice on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
